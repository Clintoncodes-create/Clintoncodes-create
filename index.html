Maximize Your Productivity by Scheduling Email Time

Tip #2: Set Aside Time for Email Management Many of us have a bad habit of checking our emails throughout the day, which can be a significant productivity drain. Instead, set aside specific times during the day to check and respond to emails. This will help you stay focused on other tasks and prevent email from taking over your day.
